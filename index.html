New Features

There are now boats in the game, but is powered by paddles and can be rode by 2 players/characters! There are now skins and you could have some for free and some for about $9.99HKD! There is now a new window in the "Menu" screen that you could change your world from Survival mode to Creative Mode and when you're in the game, you'll even have potion effects, but no actual potions.
